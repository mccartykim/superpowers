## Quick Summary: Top Recommendations

### Best Town Options (in priority order)

1. **Orono/Old Town area** - Near University of Maine
   - Median home: ~$300K
   - Excellent fiber coverage (15+ providers in Old Town)
   - College town amenities without Portland prices
   - Lower property taxes than southern Maine

2. **Waterville area** - Near Colby College
   - Median home: ~$240K (most affordable option)
   - Growing fiber infrastructure
   - 33% projected job growth
   - Good balance of affordability and amenities

3. **Brunswick area** - Near Bowdoin College
   - Higher prices but excellent infrastructure
   - Strong fiber coverage (Fidium)
   - More "established" feel, closer to Portland
